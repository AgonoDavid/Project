ASUU strike: Varsities that have announced resumption

On Friday, October 14, 2022, the Academic Staff Union of Universities announced the suspension of its eight-month strike. The strike, which commenced on Monday, February 14, 2022, led to a suspension of academic activities in Federal and State Universities across the nation. ASUU, however, in a press statement released to announce the suspension of the strike encouraged its members to resume to work.
